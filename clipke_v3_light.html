How ClipKe works
For clippers and brands alike. Transparent, performance-based, Kenyan.
For clippers
Sign up free
Phone number + connect your social channels. No follower minimum. Takes under 2 minutes.
Pick a campaign
Browse 235 live campaigns. Download the brand's raw footage from the campaign folder.
Edit and post
Cut it into a 15–60 second clip. Hook in first 2 seconds. Add required caption & hashtags. Post to specified platforms.
Submit your link
Paste your post URL into the ClipKe dashboard. We start tracking views in real time.
Brand manager reviews
After 7 days, the campaign's brand manager checks your clip — quality, brand safety, correct hashtags, no watermarks. Approved clips start earning.
Earn in KSh to M-Pesa
CPM earnings land in your balance every 7 days. Withdraw to M-Pesa, Airtel Money, or your bank. No USD, no crypto required.
For brands
Create your campaign
Set CPM rate, budget, platforms, duration, and upload your raw footage to the campaign folder.
Fund via M-Pesa Paybill
Send budget to Paybill 4042100. No USD conversion. Deposit instantly visible in your dashboard.
Clippers apply & post
Kenya's creator network discovers and clips your content across TikTok, Reels, YT Shorts, and X.
Your team reviews clips
Your brand manager gets a daily digest of submitted clips. Approve or reject each one with a single click. Rejected clips get a reason sent to the clipper.
Budget auto-disburses
CPM payouts to approved clippers are automated. Unused budget is refunded. Full analytics report on campaign close.

View tracking
ClipKe polls each platform's API every 6 hours. Views are verified, deduplicated, and fraud-checked before earnings are credited.

How views are verified
URL submitted
You paste your post link. ClipKe extracts the video/post ID and registers it for tracking.
Platform API poll
Every 6 hours the relevant API (YouTube Data API v3, TikTok Research API, Instagram Graph API) is queried for current view count.
Fraud detection
Sudden view spikes (>300% in 6hrs), bot-pattern engagement ratios, and VPN/proxy traffic are flagged. Flagged views are held pending manual review.
Brand approval window
Brand manager reviews and approves the clip within 7 days. Only approved clips count toward earnings.
Earnings credited
CPM × verified views ÷ 1000 is added to your balance every 7 days. Balance visible in real time on your dashboard.
Platform API details
▶️YouTube Data API v3✓ Active
Fetches statistics.viewCount via videos.list endpoint. Quota: 10,000 units/day. Each view check = 1 unit. We batch up to 50 IDs per call.
📱TikTok Research API⚠ Key needed
Queries /v2/video/query/ for view_count. Requires business account + Research API approval. Rate limit: 1,000 req/day.
📸Instagram Graph API⚠ Key needed
Uses /v18.0/{media-id}?fields=insights with metric plays. Requires user to grant instagram_manage_insights scope during OAuth.
𝕏X API v2⚠ Key needed
Fetches public_metrics.impression_count via GET /2/tweets/:id. Requires Basic tier ($100/mo) or higher for full impression data.
API keys & integrations
Keys are stored locally in this session only. In production, these are server-side environment variables — never exposed to the client.
